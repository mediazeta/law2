Our services

In PACTARIA LEGAL, we believe that the key to success in representing our clients lies in understanding their needs and goals, as well as having a deep knowledge of the industry in which they operate.

For this reason, we always work closely with our clients to ensure that our legal advice is fully aligned with their business objectives and market trends.

At PACTARIA LEGAL, we offer advice to our clients in a wide range of legal services, as well as extensive experience in various business sectors and activities:

Cybersecurity, crypto & blockchain

PACTARIA LEGAL was one of the pioneering firms in Asturias in providing legal services related to blockchain and crypto assets.

Among other projects, PACTARIA LEGAL has been the author of the launch of some of the most innovative cryptocurrencies in the Spanish scene, marketed through platforms such as Binance.

Additionally, we have extensive experience in advising on investments in crypto assets, having supported numerous clients in this process.

New technologies & GDPR

PACTARIA LEGAL specializes in legal services related to new technologies and data protection.

They offer a wide range of services, including web terms and conditions, software development contracts, advice on data protection, and e-commerce.

They stand out for their speed, quality, and flexibility in providing legal services.

Corporate & Mercantile

PACTARIA LEGAL recognizes the importance of legal advice for the growth of companies in the current business environment.

They offer legal services that range from initial planning to the management of agreements with clients.

Their team of experts in corporate advisory provides flexible and innovative legal consultancy, covering everything from the creation of companies to the drafting of agreements related to investments and other business aspects.

Financial & Banking

With over 15 years of experience in the banking sector, PACTARIA LEGAL provides its clients with agile and flexible advice on financing.

Their team has extensive experience in financings, refinancings, and insolvency processes, having successfully advised on multiple investment rounds.

They offer comprehensive legal services in the financial field, including the structuring, design, negotiation, and signing of agreements with public entities, financial institutions, and other private investors, instilling confidence in companies seeking liquidity.

Internationalization

& Foreign Investment

PACTARIA LEGAL provides specialized legal advice in international transactions, being approved providers of legal services by Asturex.

Their services include advice on investment projects, corporate issues, and sophisticated migratory processes such as the "Golden visa."

The firm has experience in international transactions in countries such as Mexico and the United States, supporting the success of international projects since its foundation in 2018.

Executive formation

Patricia Aira, founding partner of PACTARIA LEGAL, is passionate about education and has been an associate professor of Private International Law at the University of Oviedo since 2017.

The firm offers high-quality and affordable legal training for companies and has collaborated with recognized institutions, providing a versatile and flexible approach.
