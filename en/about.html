Pactaria Legal

Our history

  • Beginnings

    2018

    Pactaria Legal was founded by Patricia Aira in Oviedo (Principality of Asturias, Spain) on May 2, 2018, under the name "Pactaria Legal & Consulting".

  • Early years

    2019-2020

    The firm began its professional journey by specializing in the provision of legal and consulting services. It is particularly focused on providing legal services in the areas of Financial and Banking Law, Corporate Law, and International Law.

  • Specialization

    2021

    In 2021, in response to the evolution of the firm's professional activities, "Pactaria Legal & Consulting" exclusively specializes in providing legal services.

  • Growth

    2022

    Likewise, in 2022, the company managing the firm transforms into a professional partnership, PACTARIA, S.L.P., registered with the Registry of Professional Partnerships of the Bar Association of Oviedo since February 4, 2022, with registration number 142.

  • Renovation

    2023

    Also, in 2023, in response to the growth of the firm and after a process of rebranding and professional transformation, "Pactaria Legal & Consulting" becomes Pactaria Legal.

  • Nowadays

    2024

    Pactaria Legal specializes in providing exclusive legal services in Financial and Banking Law, Corporate Law, and International Law from its headquarters in Asturias.
