Our firm

Pactaria Legal

PACTARIA LEGAL is a professional legal services firm, founded in 2018 by Patricia Aira. For more than 5 years, we have been dedicated to providing high-quality professional legal services. Thanks to this, we can offer a highly efficient and effective legal service that guarantees the satisfaction of our clients.

At PACTARIA LEGAL, we specialize in providing business legal services, with special attention to the business sector in general, as well as legal advice on banking, financing (for example, film and R&D projects, among others), startups, and internationalization of companies.

Our extensive experience in these fields allows us to offer comprehensive and personalized legal advice to our clients, tailored to their specific needs and the challenges they face in their sector, marked by the quality and flexibility of PACTARIA LEGAL.
